1. Simplicity:
Keep things simple
e.g. instead of bombarding with text, use some images and keep things shorter

2. Consistency:
Keep things consistent
e.g. Keep the header consistent if there are different pages for the application

3. Reading patterns:
Use either the F-Layout or Z-Layout when designing, for better experience

4. All Platform Design:
Keep both big and small screens in mind, content should be easily readable and elements should fill out the available space
